### 2. Descriptive Statistics
I implemented the following Excel formulas to analyze the data:
- **Mean (Average):** `=AVERAGE(range)` — To find the central value.
- **Median:** `=MEDIAN(range)` — To find the middle value, minimizing the impact of outliers.
- **Mode:** `=MODE.SNGL(range)` — To identify the most frequently occurring value.
- **Standard Deviation:** `=STDEV.S(range)` — To understand the data spread/dispersion around the mean.
- **Variance:** `=VAR.S(range)` — To measure how far the numbers are spread out.
- **Min/Max & Range:** `=MIN(range)`, `=MAX(range)` — To establish the boundaries of the data.

### 3. Data Visualization
